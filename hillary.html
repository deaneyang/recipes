Hillary Rodham Clinton’s chocolate chip cookies

(Consumer Reports, October 1993)

1.5 cups unsifted flour

1 cup firmly packed light brown sugar

1 teaspoon salt

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1 teaspoon baking soda

2 eggs

1 cup solid vegetable shortening

2 cups rolled oats

1/2 cup granulated sugar

12 ounces semisweet chocolate chips

1. Preheat oven to 350°.

2. Combine flour, salt, and baking soda.

3. Beat together shortening, sugars, and vanilla until creamy. Add eggs, beating until light and fluffy. Gradually beat in the flour mixture and the rolled oats.

4. Mix in chocolate chips.

5. Drop batter by well-rounded teaspoonfuls onto cookie sheets.

6. Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until cookies are done.

7. Cool the cookies on sheets for two minutes before placing them on a wire rack for further cooling.
