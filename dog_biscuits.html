Dog Biscuit Recipe from "George and Diggety" by Maggie Stern

Ingredients
1.25 cups grated cheddar cheese
1 stick butter
1 clove crushed garlic
1 tsp. molasses
0.5 tsp salt
1/3 cup Parmesan cheese
1.5 cups whole wheat flour
1/3 cup milk

Grate cheddar cheese and, when it reaches room temperature, mix together with the butter, molasses, and salt, Parmesan cheese, and flour. Add milk and form into a ball. Chill 20 minutes. Roll onto a floured board. Cut into shapes and bake at 375 degrees for about 15 minutes or until slightly brown.
