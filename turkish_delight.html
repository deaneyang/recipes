Turkish Delight

2 cupsgranulated sugar
1 lemonjuice squeezed and strained
1 orangejuice squeezed and strained
1 orange peelsliced into thin strips
4 tablespoonsunflavored powdered gelatin
2 tablespoonsconfectioner's sugar
1 tablespoonscornstarch
1¼ cupswater
  1. Dissolve the granulated sugar in half the water over medium heat.
  2. Add the orange peel strips and juices. Bring to a boil and simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Heat remaining water and dissolve gelatin into water
  4. Mix everything together and let sit for 5 minutes.
  5. Strain mixture into a shallow dampened pan and let it set for 24 hours. (Orange peels can be left in mixture, if desired)
  6. Cut into 1 inch squares.
  7. Sift together the confectioner's sugar and cornstarch.
  8. Roll each piece in the sugar-cornstarch mixture
  9. Store pieces in a box with more of the sugar-cornstarch mixture between each layer.
