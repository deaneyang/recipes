Oatmeal with Cocoa

(Inspired by Yes, Adults Can Have Chocolate for Breakfast. Really.)
Ingredients
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup soy milk
  • 1 cup steel cut oats
  • Pinch of salt
  • 3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa
Steps
  1. Bring 1 cup soy milk and 2 cups water to a boil.
  2. Add 1 cup steel cut oats, a pinch of salt, and 2 or 3 tablespoons of coca. Do this carefully, because cocoa is a very fine powder that scatters easily.
  3. Use a whisk to mix gently at first and gradually more vigorously until cocoa is all dissolved.
  4. Turn heat down as low as you can without the flame going out, partially cover (it will boil over if you cover it completely), and cook for 15 minutes.

Cooked oatmeal can be saved in the fridge and reheated in the microwave with more soy milk added. Add a little maple syrup (or honey or sugar) and nuts, if desired.
